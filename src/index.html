About me

I am a Full-Stack developer based out of Orange County, California. My passion for software development began in a small classroom at the University of Arizona where I took part in a virtual reality course. Through that class, I was introduced to the world of programming. After months of learning game development over the summer after graduating I decided to reach out to a friend who was already working as a software engineer at the time. He introduced me to web development, and recommended I take a few courses on HTML/CSS/JavaScript. I immediately fell in love, and began learning basic web development concepts.

After a few months of doing the self-taught route, I decided on taking my skills to the next level and enrolled into a full immersion Web Development program with LearningFuze. Throughtout the program I dove deep into understanding more advanced concepts of Web Development and launched a number of web applications that utitlized technolgies such as: HTML, CSS, Javascript, React.js, Node.js, Express, SQL, and PostgreSQL
